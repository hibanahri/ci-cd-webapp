## 📝 Recommendations & Endorsements

### How to Get Recommendations
**Ask professors/mentors:**
```
Subject: LinkedIn Recommendation Request

Hi [Professor/Mentor Name],

I'm building my professional network and applying for DevOps internships. 
I would greatly appreciate a LinkedIn recommendation highlighting my technical 
skills and work ethic.

Thank you for considering my request!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

**Recommendation Template to Suggest:**
```
[Your Name] is an exceptional engineering student with strong technical skills 
in DevOps and infrastructure. During [project/course], they demonstrated:

• Strong problem-solving abilities and attention to detail
• Excellent understanding of CI/CD pipelines and automation
• Ability to learn quickly and apply new technologies
• Professional communication and documentation skills

I highly recommend [Your Name] for DevOps internship opportunities.
```

### Endorsement Strategy
- Endorse connections' skills (they often reciprocate)
- Focus on DevOps-related skills
- Endorse 5-10 people per week
- Prioritize connections in DevOps/infrastructure roles

## 🎯 Internship Application Strategy

### LinkedIn Job Search
1. **Search Filters:**
   - Job title: "DevOps Intern", "Infrastructure Intern", "Site Reliability Engineer Intern"
   - Location: [Your preferred locations]
   - Experience level: "Internship"
   - Company size: All sizes (startups to enterprises)

2. **Application Process:**
   - Customize cover letter for each application
   - Reference specific projects in your portfolio
   - Mention relevant skills from job description
   - Follow up after 1 week if no response

### Networking for Opportunities
- Attend virtual DevOps meetups
- Join LinkedIn groups: "DevOps Engineers", "Site Reliability Engineers"
- Engage with company pages (like, comment, share)
- Reach out to recruiters directly
